# Affiliate Content Prompt Library

Reusable fill-in-the-blank prompts for spinning one Amazon product into a full content set.
Source: Tenny Hainsworth thread (7 cards). Replace `[bracketed]` placeholders per run.

Defaults for this project: niche = trending Amazon products, audience = everyday consumers,
brand voice = upbeat / funny / viral, tracking ID = `hotproduct033-20`.

---

## 1 | Product Picker
> Find 25 Amazon product categories beginners can review with affiliate links. Include
> buyer intent, average price, content angle, and repeat purchase potential.

Output: products people already want.

## 2 | Review Angle
> Create 10 review angles for [product] focused on real buyer questions, problems solved,
> comparisons, and who should avoid it.

Honest angles build trust faster than hype.

## 3 | Blog Outline
> Write an SEO blog outline for Best [product type] for [audience]. Include comparison
> table, buyer guide, FAQs, and affiliate disclosure placement.

A post that can rank.

## 4 | Short Script
> Write 5 short-form video scripts reviewing [product]. Include hook, problem, feature
> demo, honest drawback, and reason to consider it.

One product → multiple content pieces.

## 5 | Comparison Copy
> Compare [product A] vs [product B] for a beginner buyer. Include pros, cons, best use
> case, and final recommendation.

Comparisons attract shoppers close to buying.

## 6 | Link System
> Create one simple link hub with product categories, short descriptions, and clear
> disclosures. Organize it by buyer problem so every post has a clean place to send
> interested viewers.

## 7 | Content Calendar
> Build a 30-day Amazon affiliate content plan for [niche]. Include daily review ideas,
> video hooks, blog topics, and Pinterest pin titles.

Multiplies each product into a month of content.

---

### Run order
Pick a product (1) → angles (2) → long-form (3) + short-form (4) → comparison (5) →
route all CTAs through the link hub (6) → schedule the month (7).
